About Me

With extensive experience in diverse markets, I turn complex datasets into clear, actionable insights that drive strategic decision-making. Mastering DAX measures and data modeling, I specialize in building dynamic, high-level reporting frameworks that elevate performance.

My passion lies in collaborating with leaders across regions and globally, ensuring data translates into value, while mentoring and empowering teams to harness the full potential of analytics.

At Teleperformance, I deliver enterprise reporting for leadership across multiple levels and geographies. My focus is dimensional modeling (star schemas), DAX that is both correct and performant, and robust ETL processes that keep refreshes stable.

Beyond day-to-day delivery, I lead Power BI adoption at scale — mentoring a community of ~80 developers and engineers, coaching on best practices, and defining KPI logic with stakeholders across diverse teams.
